About GRIET Student Branch

Founded in 2014, the GRIET Student Branch of CSI has consistently worked to make its students aware of emerging technologies. With over 500+ active members, the branch organizes hackathons, seminars, coding challenges, and collaborative tech initiatives to promote learning and innovation.
